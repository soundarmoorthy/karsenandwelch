Know Us

Karsen & Welch Cleantech Industries was set up at Chennai, the capital of state Tamil Nadu, India in the year 2001. We realized the huge potential the industry has to offer and thus ventured into a profitable business of manufacturing and supplying of Floor Cleaners, Hand Wash, Bathroom Cleaners, Furniture Polish, Fabric Cleaners, Household Cleaners, Aroma Products, Industrial Cleaners and Air Fresheners. All these items are widely used in homes, offices, factories, institutions and many other places where human activities are involved. The world is fast moving and we need to keep up with the pace, our organization has come out with tailor made solution for the complete cleaning concerns of all our esteemed clients. Clients can avail from us ready made cleaning solutions as per their needs, we have varied range and that too in huge quantities. Our decade long experience has given us enough exposure to give best products in the market, we have sufficient infrastructural facilities to meet the needs of clients in most efficient manner.

 

Our Organization runs in a very organized manner, as our business activities happen under the astute guidance of Mr. K.S. Vedhamoorthy (CEO), he continuously stands by our side and provides every support that one needs. Mr. Vedhamoorthy is very enterprising individual, this quality of his benefits the organization immensely. His in depth knowledge and keen desire in business activities have facilitated smooth business processes.

 

We conduct manufacturing at our premises and have a well organized unit for the same. Manufacturing unit is equipped with all essential equipments and raw material which helps to carry out functioning efficiently. All the raw material is tested to ensure it is not adulterated and the quality is not compromised, as one cannot expect to make desired products with sub-standard raw material. The complete testing is done at our facility where we have a separate unit for the same. Our all other units help us to carry out the business, in a direction we have set up to meet the organizational goals. They work in close coordination to derive better efficiency and mass production.

 

We work for attaining client’s satisfaction, this has also helped us to gain a good foothold in the market. Our approach has helped us to have excellent relations with the clients. Our Research and Development team never rests and gives valuable suggestions for manufacturing products of great utility for the clients. We understand the needs of clients and thus make our products available in different packaging for the complete convenience of clients, this particular act has helped us to earn great appreciation from clients.
