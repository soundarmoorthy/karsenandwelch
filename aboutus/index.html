Karsen & Welch Cleantech Industries was set up at Chennai, the capital of state Tamil Nadu, India in the year 2001. We realized the huge potential the industry has to offer and thus ventured into a profitable business of manufacturing and supplying of Floor Cleaners, Hand Wash, Bathroom Cleaners, Furniture Polish, Fabric Cleaners, Household Cleaners, Aroma Products, Industrial Cleaners and Air Fresheners. All these items are widely used in homes, offices, factories, institutions and many other places where human activities are involved. The world is fast moving and we need to keep up with the pace, our organization has come out with tailor made solution for the complete cleaning concerns of all our esteemed clients. Clients can avail from us ready made cleaning solutions as per their needs.Our decade long experience has given us enough exposure to give best products in the market, we have sufficient infrastructural facilities to meet the needs of clients in most efficient manner.
